TETRIS

Controls

  • LeftArrow Move left
  • RightArrow Move right
  • DownArrow Soft drop
  • UpArrow A Clockwise rotation
  • Z Counterclockwise rotation
  • SpaceBar Hard drop
  • 0 Send to you the first item of your items list
  • 1 → 9 Send to the player the first item of your items list

Items

  • ▲Add a row
  • ⌇⌇Shake the board during 3 seconds
  • ⏩Highly inscrease the falling speed during 2 seconds
  • ☢Remove all squares of the well
  • ↻The top is the bottom during 3 seconds

Medals

  • You did a Tetris!
  • 🔥 Highest APM of the game!
  • 💣 Highest number of items sent!
